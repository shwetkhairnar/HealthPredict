Practical Health Tips

Evidence-based recommendations for daily wellness

Nutrition Guidance

Balanced Eating

  • Fill half your plate with vegetables and fruits
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean protein sources
  • Stay hydrated with 8-10 glasses of water daily

Healthy Swaps

  • Replace sugary drinks with infused water
  • Choose baked over fried foods
  • Use herbs/spices instead of excess salt
  • Snack on nuts instead of processed snacks

Physical Activity

Weekly Routine

  • 150 minutes moderate exercise weekly
  • 2 days of strength training
  • Daily stretching/flexibility work
  • Reduce prolonged sitting

At-Home Workouts

  1. 5 min warm-up (marching in place, arm circles)
  2. 3 rounds: 10 squats, 10 push-ups, 30 sec plank
  3. 5 min cool-down with stretching

Mental Wellbeing

Stress Reduction

  • Practice deep breathing (4-7-8 technique)
  • Take short breaks during work
  • Limit news/social media consumption
  • Keep a gratitude journal

Sleep Hygiene

  • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep bedroom cool and dark
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed

Seasonal Advice

Summer Health

  • Stay hydrated in hot weather
  • Use sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Exercise during cooler hours
  • Watch for heat exhaustion signs

Winter Wellness

  • Wash hands frequently
  • Consider vitamin D supplements
  • Dress in layers outdoors
  • Get flu vaccination
